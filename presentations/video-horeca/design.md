# Green Panda — Brand Design System

## Palette

- **mint-50**: `#EEFAF8`
- **mint-100**: `#D6F2EE`
- **mint-300**: `#7DD6CB`
- **mint-500**: `#1FA8A3` (primary accent)
- **mint-700**: `#137C7A`
- **mint-900**: `#0B4B4A` (dark bg)
- **bamboo-500**: `#6CB369` (secondary)
- **bamboo-700**: `#3E7F45`
- **cream**: `#F6F4EE` (background)
- **ink**: `#0F1A1A` (text on light)
- **accent**: `#C8443E` (rare CTA)
- **gold**: `#E5B53D` (highlights)

## Typography

- **Display:** Unbounded (700–900) for headlines, labels
- **Body:** Manrope (400–700) for paragraphs, data

## Motion

- Easing: `power3.out` for entrances, `expo.inOut` for swaps
- Entrance offset: 0.2–0.4s into each scene
- Scene crossfade: 0.5s overlap

## Corners
Rounded 16–24px on cards. No sharp corners.

## Depth
Subtle: `drop-shadow(0 20px 30px rgba(15,26,26,0.15))`. No heavy shadows.

## Voice
B2B-эко, профессионально, без поп-маркетинга. Конкретные цифры, не «лучший в мире».
